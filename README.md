## üß† Revolutionary Concept

This tool redefines intelligence research by viewing intelligence as **relational capacity** rather than problem-solving hierarchy. Instead of measuring intelligence by human standards, we explore the unique cognitive capacities of trees, octopuses, dolphins, fungi, and countless other species through quantum-enhanced analysis.

### The Intelligence Framework

**Three-Dimensional Analysis:**
- **PERCEIVE** - Sensory awareness, quantum detection, adaptive learning, environmental processing
- **RELATE** - Communication patterns, symbiotic relationships, temporal coordination, cross-species interaction, ecosystem impact  
- **APPLY** - Strategic action, creative applications, collective decision-making, energy efficiency, timing wisdom (including strategic NON-response)
